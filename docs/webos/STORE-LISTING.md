## Title

**IntelHQ — Global Intelligence Dashboard**

## Short Description (≤80 chars)

Real-time global news, markets, and geopolitical intelligence on your TV.

## Long Description (≤4000 chars)

IntelHQ brings real-time global intelligence to your LG Smart TV. Monitor breaking news, financial markets, geopolitical hotspots, and more — all from your living room.

**Key Features:**

• **Live News Aggregation** — 150+ global news sources, updated every 5 minutes, with AI-powered categorization and priority ranking.

• **Interactive 3D Globe** — Navigate a real-time world map showing conflicts, earthquakes, weather events, cyber threats, and infrastructure status. Pan and zoom with your remote control.

• **Financial Markets** — Track stock indices, cryptocurrencies, commodities, and ETF flows with live price updates and sparkline charts.

• **AI Insights** — Machine-summarized intelligence briefings highlight the most significant developments across geopolitics, technology, and economics.

• **Prediction Markets** — View real-time prediction market data from Polymarket for upcoming elections, policy decisions, and global events.

• **Earthquake & Disaster Alerts** — USGS earthquake data, FIRMS fire detection, and climate anomaly tracking displayed on the globe in real-time.

• **Strategic Risk Assessment** — Country-level risk scores combining conflict data, economic indicators, governance metrics, and displacement statistics.

**Designed for TV:**

• Full D-pad navigation — browse everything with your standard LG remote
• Magic Remote support — point-and-click with the LG pointer remote
• Optimized for 1080p display — crisp text and high-contrast dark theme
• Low memory footprint — runs smoothly alongside other TV apps
• Color button shortcuts — quick access to key features

**Data Sources:**
Reuters, Al Jazeera, BBC, AP, USGS, Polymarket, CoinGecko, ACLED, UCDP, GDELT, UNHCR, World Bank, and many more.

**Languages:** English (more coming soon)
