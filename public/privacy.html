Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

IntelHQ ("we", "our", "the app") is a real-time intelligence dashboard that aggregates publicly available information from news sources, financial data providers, and open government APIs. This privacy policy applies to the IntelHQ web application and LG webOS Smart TV application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or login required
  • No email addresses, names, or contact information collected
  • No location tracking or geolocation data collected from users
  • No cookies used for tracking or advertising
  • No user-generated content stored on our servers

3. Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device only (using browser localStorage and IndexedDB):

  • Your panel layout preferences
  • Map layer toggle states
  • Custom monitor configurations
  • Cached news and market data for offline viewing
  • Language preference
  • Theme preference (dark/light)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser or TV app settings.

4. Analytics

The web version uses Vercel Analytics for anonymous, aggregated usage metrics (page views, performance timing). No personally identifiable information is collected. The TV app does not include analytics.

5. Error Reporting

The app uses Sentry for error tracking. When an error occurs, technical information about the error is sent (stack trace, browser version, error message). No personal data is included.

6. Third-Party Data Sources

The app fetches publicly available data from third-party APIs including but not limited to: USGS (earthquakes), CoinGecko (crypto), Polymarket (predictions), ACLED (conflicts), GDELT (news), MapTiler (map tiles). Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We do not share any user data with these services.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Data Security

All API communications use HTTPS encryption. The app does not transmit or store any sensitive personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@intelhq.io.
